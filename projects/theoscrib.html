Summary

Theo's Crib is a passion project of mine which took about a year to build, and is continuously maintained.

This is a website I developed to keep connected with friends and to keep some amount of continuity between video games we play. Users can create and post to threads, link their Steam accounts to show off their achievements, and link video game accounts to their Theo's Crib accounts for Theo's Crib specific servers.

For example, users can connect their Minecraft account to their Theo's Crib account and then receive special Theo's Crib achievements by playing on the server.

The website can be found at https://www.theoscrib.net, however, this website is invite only. In order to register an account you must be given a registration key.
